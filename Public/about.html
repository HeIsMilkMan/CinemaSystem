About Us

Welcome to Decade Cinemas, the best place to experience the latest movies in Letterkenny, Co. Donegal. We pride ourselves on providing our customers with the highest quality cinematic experience, complete with state-of-the-art sound and visual technology, comfortable seating, and a variety of snacks and refreshments to enjoy during the movie.

Our theater boasts a variety of amenities to make your visit a truly enjoyable one. All of our screens feature Dolby Digital surround sound and crystal clear digital projection, ensuring that you won't miss a single moment of your favorite movie. We offer a variety of seating options, from standard seats to luxurious recliners, so you can choose the perfect spot to watch your film.

We also have a fully stocked concession stand with all of your favorite snacks and refreshments, including popcorn, sweets, soda, and more. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is always happy to help you select the perfect treat to enjoy during your movie.

With a constantly rotating selection of the latest blockbusters and timeless classics, there's something for everyone at Decade Cinemas. Visit us today and see the difference for yourself!
